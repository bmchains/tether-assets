Why it matters

Crypto still feels too technical for everyday users.

Wallet addresses, gas fees, scams, seed phrases, and chain switching create friction. The redesigned layout turns that message into a stronger narrative with cleaner content grouping and a more product-led feel.

The problem

Wallets and seed phrases Gas fees and networks Bridges and chain switching Scams and risky transactions Cashing out to bank accounts Fear of expensive mistakes

The solution

A guided digital asset experience shaped around AI assistance, clearer education, safer transaction flows, and a friendlier interface for non-technical users.

“How do I receive crypto?” “Which network has the lowest fees?” “Move my TetherAsset to savings.” “Convert my crypto to cash.”
